2. Problem

Strength training is one of the fastest-growing forms of fitness globally. Yet, lifters still rely on generic sneakers or outdated silhouettes like Converse — neither of which are designed to support the biomechanics of lifting. The market has evolved with wrist wraps, belts, and gear — but not footwear.

None of the major footwear giants—Nike, Adidas—currently offer true training shoes. What they promote as gym shoes are mostly padded jogging shoes. These lack the flat sole, grip, and structure required for serious lifts, making them impractical for actual strength training. A massive market segment remains underserved.

3. Solution

Stabl is building purpose-engineered footwear for lifters who don’t compromise.

Like cleats are for football, we aim to be for the gym. In time, we shall name this niche as wearing “Stabl’s to the gym.”

Our trainers are designed for maximum ground grip, stability, and ankle support — empowering better form and stronger lifts.
